ArbTrust is an Iidentity Verification Decentralized application, which enables users to collect an AuthenticVerifiable Stamp(AVS) in form of NFT.

ArbTrust, built on the Arbitrum network and leveraging artificial intelligence, will serve as a virtual barrier, which keeps out bots and bad actors, allowing only verifed and trustworthy individuals into the blockchain ecosystem without exposing personal or sensitive information.
